You're using containerized instances to quickly deploy your fleet management solution into new customer regions. You're looking for a container orchestration platform. This platform should make it simple to develop, deploy, and manage containerized applications.

AKS manages your hosted Kubernetes environment and makes it simple to develop, deploy, and managed containerized applications in Azure. You don't need to manage any of the clusters infrastructures and you only pay for what you use. It streamlines the installation process and takes care of most of the underlying cluster management tasks.

AKS offers several features that enhances the standard Kubernetes offering. It allows you to use familiar Kubernetes tools, development environments, and existing deployment workflows.

As well, AKS is part of Azure and can be integrated with other Azure services to extend your product or exchange data with other services.
