# Exercise - Create an Azure Account

In this exercise, you will create free Azure account. You need a valid credit card to carry out this exercise; however, your card will not be charged.

## Sign up for a Free Account

To sign up for a free Azure account, carry out the following steps:

1. In a web browser, navigate to [azure.microsoft.com](https://azure.microsoft.com) and click **Start for free**.

2. On the **Create your Azure free account today** page, click **Start for free**.

3. On the **About you** page, select your correct country or region, then enter your first and last names, along with your email address and phone number. Depending on your country, you may see additional fields, such as VAT number. Click **Next** to continue.

![New Account 1](../images/2-new-account-1.png)

4. On the Identity verification by phone screen, select your country code and type in the number of a phone to which you have immediate access.

![New Account 2](../images/2-new-account-2.png)

5. You have the option of text or callback to obtain a verification code. Click the relevant button, type the code in to the **Verification code** box, and click **Verify code**.

![New Account 3](../images/2-new-account-3.png)

6. If the verification code is correct, you will now be asked to enter details of a valid credit card. Enter the card number, the expiration date, the CVV number, your name as it is written on the card, the address to which the card is registered, along with the post or zip code, then click **Next**.

![New Account 4](../images/2-new-account-4.png)

7. Finally, check the box to accept the subscription agreement, privacy statement, and communications policy. The second checkbox is optional. Now click **Sign up**.

![New Account 5](../images/2-new-account-5.png)

8. You have now successfully set up a free account and you will be taken to the Portal home page.

![Portal Home Page](../images/2-azure-portal-home.png)
