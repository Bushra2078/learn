
## Plan for SaaS business

In this training module, you learned about the main SaaS concepts, such as:

- Reasons why SaaS model is so popular, such as high customer demand for the consumption model, faster time to market for new functionality, and better security.
- The LAER consumption model, and why adoption is at its core. How adoption affects churn and company financial results.
- Difference between SLA and SLOs, and how to define simple SLOs for your solution.
- Available pricing model options for SaaS service.

The first stage for getting started with your SaaS business is to make your business-level decisions.

- What problem are you trying to solve? 
- How do you plan to solve the problem?
- What is your pricing model?
- Who are your customers and how will they interact with your solution?

The answers to these questions provide the requirements for your SaaS solution, as when defining the target market segment for the Contoso SaaS solution. The approach each company takes depends on many factors, including the current stage of business. Some startups or new software companies still need to find product market fit and define a sustainable business model that's ready for growth and scale.
