## Introduction

*Software as a service (SaaS)* allows users to connect to and use cloud-based apps over the Internet. Common examples are email, calendaring, and office tools (such as Microsoft Office 365 or Azure Cloud).

In this module, you are going to focus on SaaS foundations, so that you have a solid guide to create your own SaaS company and solution.

## Learning objectives

Upon completion of this module, you will be able to:

- Identify the most common reasons customers prefer SaaS model and solutions.
- Describe the difference between license-based software and SaaS.
- Describe the consumption-based model.
- Describe the difference between SLA and SLO and define simple SLOs for your solution.
- Identify suitable pricing model for your SaaS service

## Prerequisites

- Basic familiarity with IT terms and concepts
