In this module, we're going to look at a range of revenue models and discuss how they can be applied to different types of startups.

We're also going to cover strategies for making early sales and discuss why it's important to focus on ideal first customers and early adopters rather than aim to acquire a large number of users at the outset.

## Learning objectives

By the end of this module, you'll know:

- The different types of revenue models and how to pick one that makes sense for your company.
- How to decide which customers to target when you first go to market.
- How to find and convert your first customers.
- How to establish basic sales processes that will help you grow sales beyond the first few customers.
