The goal of this module was to outline some steps that you can take to identify a good name for your startup, and then use this name as the foundation for a strong company brand. We used eight startup naming principles as a guide to help you choose a good name for your company. 

We discussed the importance of building and strengthening your personal brand as a founder. We also covered how you can use your personal brand to add credibility to your company's brand.
