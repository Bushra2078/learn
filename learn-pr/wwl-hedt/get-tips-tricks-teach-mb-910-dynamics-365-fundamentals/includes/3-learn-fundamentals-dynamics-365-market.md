### Module 1 overview 

As you begin to teach this module, get familiar with what the students will learn during the module. In this module students will learn the Dynamics 365 customer engagement applications that are available and understand the features that are common to all the individual Dynamics 365 customer engagement applications.  

This module consists of four lessons: 

- **Lesson 1** - Get introduced to the customer engagement apps 

- **Lesson 2** - Explore Dynamics 365 Marketing 

- **Lesson 3** - Describe Dynamics 365 Marketing capabilities 

- **Lesson 4** - Review additional marketing apps

### Module 1 tips and tricks 

- Focus on identifying and describing what each of the services are and what they do 

- Understand Microsoft Dataverse is the underlying theme for the entire Power Platform 

- Use the lab environment to demonstrate the process during the instructional portion of the class
