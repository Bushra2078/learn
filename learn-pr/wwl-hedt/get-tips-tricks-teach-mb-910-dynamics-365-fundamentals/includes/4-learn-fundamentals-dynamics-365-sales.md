### Module 2 overview 

As you begin to teach this module, get familiar with what the students will learn during the module. In this module, students will learn Dynamics 365 Sales and how organizations can use it to support sales scenarios. Additionally, students will learn how this functionality helps businesses make better decisions quickly by bringing together a complete and comprehensive solution. 

This module consists of three lessons: 

- **Lesson 1** - Examine Dynamics 365 Sales 

- **Lesson 2** - Manage the sales lifecycle with Dynamics 365 Sales 

- **Lesson 3** - Review additional sales apps

### Module 2 tips and tricks 

- Focus on the terminology around the sales lifecycle 

 - Consider illustrating or using graphics provided in the slides 

- Review the reasons why a lead or opportunity would be qualified and disqualified 

- Focus on the difference between a quote and an invoice 

- Utilize sales insights and LinkedIn Sales Navigator to focus on the opportunities most likely to close 

- Leverage the lab environment as much as possible to demonstrate the capabilities of Dynamics 365 Sales
