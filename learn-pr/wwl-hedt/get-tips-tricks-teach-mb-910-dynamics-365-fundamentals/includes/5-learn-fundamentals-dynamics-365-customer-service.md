### Module 3 overview

As you begin to teach this module, get familiar with what the students will learn during the module. In this module, students will learn about Dynamics 365 Customer Service and how organizations can use it to support their customers.  

This module consists of three lessons: 

- **Lesson 1** - Explore Dynamics 365 Customer Service 

- **Lesson 2** - Describe Dynamics 365 Customer Service components 

- **Lesson 3** - Review other customer service apps 

> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/videoplayer/embed/RE4UbRf]

### Module 3 tips and tricks 

- Focus on customer service terminology 
 - Consider using a story to introduce and explain the terminology 
- Describe the capabilities of Dynamics 365 throughout the case lifecycle 
- Review considerations when designing customer service routes 
- Explain the relationship between service level agreements and the customer service lifecycle 
- Leverage the lab environment for managing tickets and open cases
