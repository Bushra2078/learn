### Module 1 overview 

As you begin to teach this module, get familiar with what the students will learn during the module. In this module, students will learn the general capabilities the finance and operations apps share, including built-in reporting and integration with other Microsoft products, such as Microsoft Excel, Power BI, Word, and Teams. 

This module consists of five lessons: 

- **Lesson 1** - Get introduced to the finance and operations apps 

- **Lesson 2** - Identify general capabilities of Dynamics 365 Supply Chain Management 

- **Lesson 3** - Explore warehouse and inventory management capabilities 

- **Lesson 4** - Explore manufacturing strategies 

- **Lesson 5** - Explore enterprise asset management capabilities

### Module 1 tips and tricks 

- Explain the concept of an ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) system and why businesses are dependent on them 

 - Consider creating a fictitious business to use throughout the course as a case study, this business can be referred to throughout the course modules as an example 

- Describe how the manufacturing type will determine the product planning stage 

- Explore the tools available to your management team for optimizing production floors 

- Focus on the life cycle processes (production, purchasing, etc.) 

 - Students should recognize where different tasks or activities occur in the process 

- Use the lab environment to demonstrate the process during the instructional portion of the class
