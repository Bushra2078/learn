As you are preparing to teach this module, get familiar with what the students will learn during the module. In this module, students will learn about compliance solutions at Microsoft. Topics covered will include Compliance Center, Information protection and governance in Microsoft 365, Insider Risk, audit, and eDiscovery solutions. Also covered are Azure resources governance capabilities. 

This module consists of five lessons: 

- **Lesson 1** – Describe the capabilities of Microsoft compliance solutions 

- **Lesson 2** – Describe the information protection and governance capabilities of Microsoft 365 

- **Lesson 3** – Describe the insider risk capabilities in Microsoft 365 

- **Lesson 4** – Describe the eDiscovery and audit capabilities of Microsoft 365 

- **Lesson 5** – Describe the resource governance capabilities in Azure

### Module 4 tips and tricks 

- Start by explaining the importance of compliance 

- Include examples of Regulations (Global, Regional, Industry, Local) 

- Link Compliance Center, Compliance Manager, and Compliance Score, going from general examples to specific examples to connect the purpose of the tools 

- Create a narrative linking Lessons 1, 2, and 5 

- Use real-life scenarios to complement the theory
