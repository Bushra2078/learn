As you are preparing to teach this module, get familiar with what the students will learn during the module – the concepts and methodologies they will be working through. In this module students will learn about core concepts, principles, and methodologies that are foundational to security, compliance, and identity solutions, including Zero-Trust, shared responsibility, our privacy principles, and more.  

This module consists of two lessons: 

**Lesson 1**– Describe security and compliance concepts and methodologies 

**Lesson 2** – Describe identity concepts

### Module 1 tips and tricks

- Go from general to specifics 
- Save Zero Trust and Cloud Adoption Frameworks for last 
- Thoroughly explain Directory Services 
- When explaining Zero Trust, explain each of the pillars 
 - Give real-world examples and lead discussions around each pillar 
 - Utilize videos on Zero Trust on the Microsoft website 
 - Do what you can do to make these concepts real, research the latest statistics or current headlines in the news 
- Make sure to show students the Cloud Adoption Frameworks 
- Microsoft Whiteboard is very useful when explaining Federated Services 
 - Have a visual to show how identity can be used to authenticate on different sites or services
