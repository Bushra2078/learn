As you are preparing to teach this module, get familiar with what the students will learn during the module. In this module, students will learn about Azure Active Directory (AD) services and identity principals, secure authentication, access management capabilities, as well as identity protection and governance. 

This module consists of four lessons: 

- **Lesson 1** – Describe the services and identity types of Azure AD 

- **Lesson 2** – Describe the authentication capabilities of Azure AD 

- **Lesson 3** – Describe the access management capabilities of Azure AD 

- **Lesson 4** – Describe the identity protection and governance capabilities of Azure AD

### Module 2 tips and tricks 

- Comprehensively explain Azure Active Directory at a high level 

 - Find additional information about [Azure Active Directory](https://aka.ms/aad)  

- Demos provide real-life examples to theories covered 

 - Run through the demos prior to teaching the module 

- Explain the elements of Multifactor Authentication 

 - Consider showing a video about how people share their passwords 

- Describe the different governance tools 

 - Consider creating a fictional company with the different scenarios and allow students the opportunity to figure out how to apply the governance tools 

 - Find additional information about [Customer and Partner Success Stories](https://aka.ms/customerstories)
