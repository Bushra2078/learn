Attack Surface Reduction (ASR) is hardening the places where a threat is likely to attack.  As a Security Analyst, it is your role to understand the protection options and provide recommendations.   While you are performing alert investigations, it is important to know the events generated by ASR on the host, which might provide forensics evidence.
