Keeping up with security legislation and regulatory requirements can be daunting. Microsoft helps you to keep abreast of relevant guidelines by providing information you need. In this module, you will be introduced to Microsoft’s privacy principles and the Service Trust Portal. You will learn where to find compliance documentation relevant to your geographic locations, and industry.

In this module, you will learn how to:

- Describe Microsoft’s privacy principles.
- Explore the Service Trust Portal.
- Know where to go to find and review Azure compliance documentation.
