Keeping up with security legislation and regulatory requirements can be daunting. Microsoft helps you to keep abreast of relevant guidelines by providing the information you need. In this module, you'll be introduced to Microsoft’s privacy principles and the Service Trust Portal. You'll learn where to find compliance documentation relevant to your geographic locations and industry.

In this module, you'll learn how to:

- Describe Microsoft’s privacy principles.
- Explore the Service Trust Portal.
- Know where to go to find and review Azure compliance documentation.
