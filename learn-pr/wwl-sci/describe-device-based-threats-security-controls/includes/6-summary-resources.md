You've learned that devices can be the key to vital information on individuals and organizations, and that cybercriminals target devices to gain unauthorized access to data. Cybercriminals can use different means to compromise devices. To protect data, and so protect people and organizations, you've learned that you can protect devices through cybersecurity measures that help you to achieve and maintain the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data.

- Describe what your device knows about you
- Describe how devices and become cybersecurity threats
- Describe how to mitigate against device-related threats
