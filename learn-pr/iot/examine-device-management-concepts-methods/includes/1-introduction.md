Azure IoT device management covers the full lifecycle of a device from device initialization, production usage, through device retirement.

Suppose you have joined an engineering team that is implementing an IoT solution for the company. The company needs your team to manage the IoT devices that have been deployed in the field. Understanding the various approaches to Azure IoT device management and when each should be applied helps to prepare the team for their upcoming device management responsibilities. You decide to examine the Azure IoT device management patterns and approaches that are available.

In this module, you will:

- Review IoT device management concepts.
- Examine IoT device management patterns.
- Examine IoT device configuration options using device twins and direct methods.

By the end of this module, you'll be able to describe the approaches to Azure IoT device management and when each should be applied.
