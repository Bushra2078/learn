Starting notes: 
"    * Use:
        * You're already representing devices in IoT Hub
        * You want to query environment on-demand or over time
        * You want to see 2D or 3D visual of your environment
        * You want to pull in business data to contextualize your IoT data
    * Don't use:
        * You require integrated use of Commands back to the IoT devices
        * You don't want to use other Azure services
        * You don't want to convert your industry models to DTDL"
