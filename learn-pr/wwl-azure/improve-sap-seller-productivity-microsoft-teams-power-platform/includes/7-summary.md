In this module, you learned how Microsoft Teams and Power Automate can help you improve everyday activities, such as these:

- Enable the SAP Sales and Service Core Teams app to support productivity and collaboration
- Create Power Automate flows to identify urgent customer emails
- Explore AI Builder to create custom data extraction actions
- Explore Power Automate flows to embed email attachment details into Adaptive Cards
- Share order details for approval in Microsoft Teams and send to SAP
