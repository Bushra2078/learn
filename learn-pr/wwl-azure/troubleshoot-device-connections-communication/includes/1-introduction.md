Device connections and communication are the two features of an IoT solution that often require troubleshooting.

Suppose you have joined a team that is implementing an Azure IoT solution for the company. The company needs your team to troubleshoot device connection and communication issues for their IoT solution. Understanding the types of issues that can impact device connections/communication and how to avoid them will help the team to support the company's reliability goals. You decide to examine best practice and guidance documentation related to device connections and communication.

In this module, you will:

 -  Examine device connection best practices.
 -  Examine the device communication troubleshooting guide.

By the end of this module, you will be able to describe the common device connection and communication issues and the best practices that can be used to avoid them.
