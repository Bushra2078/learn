## Scenario

You need to ensure your company can maintain business continuity. Needs span from small outages, to full disaster recovery of SAP HANA on Azure (Large Instances). You define the strategy with your team and implement storage snapshots and operating system backups. Then, with your team, you establish the requirements and configure HANA Large Instances disaster recovery and backup and restore.

## Learning objectives

You will:

 -  Explore snapshot strategies.
 -  Back up and restore the operating system for Type II SKUs.
 -  Implement disaster recovery on HANA Large Instances.
 -  Backup and restore SAP HANA on Azure (Large Instances).
