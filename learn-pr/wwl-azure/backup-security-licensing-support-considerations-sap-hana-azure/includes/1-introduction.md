You are responsible for the overall SAP HANA on Azure (Large Instances) migration, and several teams involved need to plan for how backups and snapshots will be handled and all of the considerations. You provide the teams details of the unique solutions that apply to SAP HANA on Azure Large Instances backup. Additionally, you need to ensure the teams understand the licensing for SAP HANA on Azure Large Instances and where the boundaries of responsibilities are between the teams in your company and the Azure support team.

## Learning objectives

You will:

 -  Explore backup considerations for SAP HANA on Azure (Large Instances).
 -  Explore storage snapshots of SAP HANA on Azure (Large Instances).
 -  Examine storage snapshot considerations.
 -  Examine SAP HANA on Azure (Large Instances) security considerations.
 -  Explore SAP HANA on Azure (Large Instances) licensing considerations.
 -  Explore SAP HANA on Azure (Large Instances) support considerations.
 -  Explore the division of management responsibilities.
