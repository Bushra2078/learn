## Scenario

Planning and implementing high availability and disaster recovery for SAP HANA on Azure (Large Instances) requires unique solutions. Your company has asked you to drive planning for disaster recovery for SAP on HANA Large Instances with the deployment team.

## Learning objectives

You will:

 -  Explore SAP HANA on Azure (Large Instances) high availability and disaster recovery.
 -  Explore network considerations for disaster recovery with SAP HANA on Azure (Large Instances).
 -  Implement disaster recovery on HANA Large Instances.
 -  Prepare the disaster recovery scenario.
 -  Fail over to a disaster recovery site.
 -  Failback from a disaster recovery to a production site.
