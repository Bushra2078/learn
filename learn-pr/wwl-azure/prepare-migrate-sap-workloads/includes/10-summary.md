In this module, you reviewed a checklist designed to assist organizations migrate SAP NetWeaver, S/4HANA, and Hybris applications to Azure. Remember that for the duration of the project it's important for organizations to review their progress against the checklist. Doing so allows for detection of small issues along the way and quickly re-engineer and test the necessary changes. You can now apply these learnings to future projects when planning how to migrate applications to Azure.
