In this module, we explored moving an on-premises system to the cloud. Making a move of this scope takes planning, resources, and a good understanding of the options involved. Overall, moving to the cloud can help you modernize your infrastructure, make management of your resources easier, and can save your organization money in the long term.

In this module, you learned how to:

* Describe why moving your app to Azure can be beneficial
* Assess your app and estimate the costs of migration
* Identify migration dependencies and priorities
* Describe post-migration cost optimization and security
