As you are preparing to teach this module, get familiar with what the students will learn during the module. In this module, students will learn about different artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning and Internet of Things (IoT) services, management and organizational tools in Azure, and serverless technology. Students will analyze decision criteria that experts use to select the right service for a given scenario. 

This module consists of six lessons: 

- **Lesson 1** – Choose the best AI service for your needs 

- **Lesson 2** – Choose the best tools to help organizations build better solutions 

- **Lesson 3** – Choose the best monitoring service for visibility, insight, and outage mitigation 

- **Lesson 4** – Choose the best tools for managing and configuring your Azure environment 

- **Lesson 5** – Choose the best Azure serverless technology for your business scenario 

- **Lesson 6** – Choose the best Azure IoT service for your application
