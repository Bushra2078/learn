1. Go to virtual machine and copy the Public IP address.

   [![vm-overview](../media/vm-overview.png)](../media/vm-overview.png)

2. Open your browser, connect to **http://<replace_your_public_IP_address>:8181**

3. Click on Home and navigate to scenario library.

   [![vision-on-edge](../media/vision-on-edge.png)](../media/vision-on-edge.png)

   [![vision-edge-scenario-library](../media/vision-edge-scenario-library.png)](../media/vision-edge-scenario-library.png)

4. Click on **Defect detection** scenario.

   [![defect-detection](../media/defect-detection.png)](../media/defect-detection.png)

5. Click on **Deploy** to deploy the scenario.

   [![deploy-scenario](../media/deploy-scenario.png)](../media/deploy-scenario.png)

6. Your deployment will start to detect defects.

   [![defect-detect](../media/defect-detect.png)](../media/defect-detect.png)
