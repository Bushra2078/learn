The Azure Health Bot service enables you to deliver services such as checking for symptoms, booking appointments and performing triage seamlessly. Having a basic understanding of LUIS and its related concepts are essential in implementing language understanding in your Azure Health Bot.

Now that you have reviewed this module, you should:

- Have a basic understanding of LUIS and concepts related to it
- Understand the importance of different language models
- Know how to create Language models on the Azure Health Bot portal
- Know how to integrate a LUIS App to the Azure Health Bot
