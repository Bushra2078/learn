This module focused on the interview process and how to master it.

First, you learned how to prepare for various types of interviews. There are various platforms and links you can read on to ensure you practice and build your skill set.

Then you focused on the interview and taught how to prepare for it. Being interviewed can be a bit stressful but practice will help you feel more comfortable. Knowing what the interviewers expect should make this experience a bit more manageable.

Lastly you learned interview guidelines, how to leave a lasting good impression and how to approach the topic of salary negotiation.
