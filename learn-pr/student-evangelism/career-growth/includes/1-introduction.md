## Scenario: You became an intern, now to make the most of your time in the internship

You're new in an organization full of talented developers and engineers of all types. A bewildering array of colleagues greets you on your first day. You meet graphic designers, administrators, managers, software testers, and many developers. You are introduced to your team, and feel like you are ready to start work. But beyond the excitement of the first day, how will you grow your career as a new intern and turn this opportunity into a full-time role? In this module, you'll learn some strategies on how to 'growth-hack' your career.

> Learn more about the concept of [growth-hacking](https://www.linkedin.com/learning/growth-hacking-foundations/grow-your-business-with-growth-marketing?u=3322).

## What will you learn?

By the end of this module, you'll be able to:

- Grow your network to increase your opportunities and influence
- Build trust as a new employee and increase the likelihood of getting a full-time offer
- Apply techniques to continue your professional growth

## What is the main goal?

By the end of this session, you'll be able to list strategies that you will be able to take to maximize your chances to go from being an intern to be offered a full-time employment.
