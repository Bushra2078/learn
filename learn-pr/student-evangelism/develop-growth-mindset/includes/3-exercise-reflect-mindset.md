In this exercise, you'll learn to reflect on your mindset. It's a good idea to note your answers on a device or on paper.

## Reflect on your mindset

Use the following prompts to reflect on your own mindset: 

- In which areas of your life do you think you have a growth mindset?

- In which areas do you think you have a fixed mindset?

- Think about the moments in your life when your mindset influenced the way you approached learning.

- Are there areas where you shifted from a fixed mindset to a growth mindset? What prompted the change?

- What kind of mindset do you have when it comes to computer science and technology?
