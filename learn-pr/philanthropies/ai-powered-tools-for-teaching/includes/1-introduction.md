This module consists of example activities that demonstrate how to integrate AI-powered tools into your learning environment. It serves as a practical guide for those who are keen to explore and practice new AI skills. 

Imagine you are a trainer who is eager to enhance your learners' experience with the help of AI. With M365, you have access to a variety of AI tools and services. These resources can be instrumental in helping you create engaging and personalized activities that cater to the unique needs of your learners.

By the end of this module, you will not only understand AI-powered activities, but also acquire the skills to create such activities for your own learning environment. These activities will utilize Sway and Speaker Coach in PowerPoint.
