In your global retail company, you had a disparate set of database technologies that resulted from independent database administration departments choosing different technologies and the acquisition of other businesses, which brought their own systems. You wanted to both standardize and modernize your data estate. By undertaking the five stages of a Data Platform Modernization project, you enabled an efficient migration to cloud database systems with the lowest total cost of ownership.

Unless you execute your modernization project carefully and systematically, they can go badly wrong and impact poorly on user productivity, customer communications, and other business processes. By using the TCO Calculator and completing the five stage of Data Platform Modernization thoroughly, you can ensure that your project runs smoothly and your move to a modern cloud data estate starts to yield benefits quickly.
