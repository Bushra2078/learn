In this module, you learned how the Video Indexer extracts insights from video files. You can use the Video Indexer Portal to search, edit, and manage your videos. You now know how to use the Video Indexer API to upload, index, retrieve insights, and search. With this knowledge you can explore more of what the API has to offer, such as customizing models used by the service and providing your own transcripts.

Now that you have concluded this module, you should know how to:

- Create a Video Indexer account and subscribe to the API.
- Upload and index videos with the API.
- Examine the Video Indexer insights.
- Find moments in video files using search.
- Detect scenes, shots, and keyframes in your videos.
- View and edit insights related to your videos.
