To perform data science, it's important to understand the underlying structure and extract meaningful insights from the data. These insights can guide you in choosing the appropriate model that best fit the data.

Through data exploration, you have the ability to identify inaccuracies in the data that require rectification, or potentially uncover intriguing occurrences that warrant additional investigation.

Data exploration is like a preliminary investigation of the data, setting the stage for all subsequent steps in the data science process.
