Relational databases are widely used for building real world applications. Understanding the characteristics of relational data is important. A relational database is based on tables. You can run many database management systems on-premises and in the cloud.

In this module you have learned:

- The characteristics of relational data
- What are tables, indexes and views
- The various relational data workload offerings available in Azure.
