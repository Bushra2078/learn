As you are preparing to teach this module, get familiar with what the students will learn during the module. In this module, students will learn how to use the Computer Vision service to read text in images and documents, and how to use the Form Recognizer service to extract data from forms. 

This module consists of two lessons: 

- **Lesson 1** – Reading text with Computer Vision service 

- **Lesson 2** – Extracting information from forms with the Form Recognizer service
