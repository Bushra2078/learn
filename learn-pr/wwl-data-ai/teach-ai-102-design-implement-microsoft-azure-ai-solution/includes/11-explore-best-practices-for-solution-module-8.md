As you are preparing to teach this module, get familiar with what the students will learn during the module. In this module, students will learn how to use the Computer Vision service to analyze images, and the Video Analyzer service to analyze videos. 

This module consists of two lessons: 

- **Lesson 1** – Analyzing images 

- **Lesson 2** – Analyzing videos

### Module 8 Tips and Tricks 

- Explain Computer Vision tools are often integrated with other cognitive services to provide part of a larger solution 
- Discuss how Computer Vision is used for analysis NOT for recognition. Face recognition, object detection, and classification will be presented in Module 9 and Module 10 
- Consider presenting labs as demonstrations
