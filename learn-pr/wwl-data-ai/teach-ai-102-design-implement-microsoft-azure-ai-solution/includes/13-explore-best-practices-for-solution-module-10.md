As you are preparing to teach this module, get familiar with what the students will learn during the module. In this module, students will learn how to use the Computer Vision and Face services to detect, analyze, and recognize faces. 

This module consists of two lessons: 

- **Lesson 1** – Detecting faces with the Computer Vision service 

- **Lesson 2** – Using the Face service 

> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/videoplayer/embed/RE4XJoh]  

### Module 10 Tips and Tricks 

- Explain how Computer Vision service is limited in its face recognition abilities 
  - Discuss the difference in recognizing faces in an image vs recognizing objects in an image 
- Explore how Face service does facial analysis 
  - Consider using online tools in which students can participate in facial analysis activities or demonstrations 
  - Consider presenting labs as demonstrations
