As you are preparing to teach this module, get familiar with what the students will learn during the module. In this module, students will learn how to use the Speech service to recognize, synthesize, and translate speech. 

This module consists of two lessons: 

- **Lesson 1** – Speech recognition and synthesis 

- **Lesson 2** – Speech translation 

> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/videoplayer/embed/RE4XJog] 

### Module 4 Tips and Tricks 

- Emphasize the difference between speech recognition and text recognition 
  - Discuss with students what takes place in each of these processes 
- Explain that translation can go from speech to text or text to speech 
- Understand that with Text to Speech you choose the “Voice”
