There are many aspects to managing a LUIS app.  Creating an app, adding intents, entities, and utterances are all part of the app creation portion of the LUIS lifecycle.  Likewise, training and publishing are two more aspects of the lifecycle. Once your LUIS app is published, you can transition to a manage and maintain scenario.

## Learning objectives

In this module you will learn to:

- Manage authoring and runtime keys
- Manage versioning
- Manage the data in your LUIS app
- Monitor and improve prediction accuracy
- Utilize patterns

## Pre-requisites

- Knowledge of navigating the Azure portal
- Knowledge of at least one programming language such as C#, Python, or JavaScript
- Basic understanding of REST APIs and JSON
