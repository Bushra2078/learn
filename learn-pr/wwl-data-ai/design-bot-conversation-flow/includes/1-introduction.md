Developing a bot is similar to developing other software applications from the perspective of a lifecycle. Your bot application should follow a general lifecycle model for creation and improvement. This module focuses on the design considerations that help make your bot successful.

## Learning objectives

In this module, you will learn how to:

- Design bot conversation flow
- Design bot navigation
- Design the user experience
- Effectively hand-off to humans

## Pre-requisites

- Active Azure account
- Knowledge of Azure portal navigation
- Knowledge of at least one programming language (C#, Python)
