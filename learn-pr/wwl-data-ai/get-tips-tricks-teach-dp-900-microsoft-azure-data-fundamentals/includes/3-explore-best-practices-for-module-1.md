As you are preparing to teach this module, get familiar with what the students will learn during the module – the concepts and methodologies they will be working through. In this module students will explore relational data offerings, provisioning and deploying relational databases, and querying relational data through cloud data solutions with Microsoft Azure. Students will also review common tasks typically performed by these job roles and the types of tools they use.   

This module consists of five lessons: 

- **Lesson 1** – Explore core data concepts 

- **Lesson 2** – Explore roles and responsibilities in the world of data 

- **Lesson 3** – Describe concepts of relational data 

- **Lesson 4** – Explore concepts of non-relational data 

- **Lesson 5** – Explore concepts of data analytics

### Module 1 Tips and Tricks 

- Consider timing as this is the longest module in the course 

- Consider this module as your road map for the other three modules 

- Focus on definitions and terminology 

- Utilize storytelling and examples 

- Teach concepts like Services at a high level. Avoid going into detail as there will be more time for this in the next modules
