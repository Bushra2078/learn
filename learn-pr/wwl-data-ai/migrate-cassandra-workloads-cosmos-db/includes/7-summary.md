In this module, you discovered the benefits of migrating a Cassandra database to Azure Cosmos DB, you learned how to plan a Cassandra to Cosmos DB migration, you viewed the steps to perform a migration, and you learned the post migration steps to connect your application and optimize your database.

## Takeaways

In this module you learned how to:

- List the benefits of migrating a Cassandra database to Azure Cosmos DB.
- Plan a Cassandra to Cosmos DB migration.
- Explain the steps to perform a migration.
- List the post migration steps to connect your application and optimize your database.
