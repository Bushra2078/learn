As you're preparing to teach this module, get familiar with what the students will learn during the module. In this module, students will learn about Natural Language Processing (NLP) and the different ways that NLP works including text analysis, translation, speech to text and text to speech services, and many more.

This module consists of two lessons: 

- **Lesson 1** – Introduction to Natural Language Processing
- **Lesson 2** – Using Natural Language Processing Services

### Module 4 Tips and Tricks

- Ensure your microphone is working if you're going to demonstrate the Speech-to-Text services
- Use the [AI demos](https://aka.ms/demosai) to showcase the Text Analytics feature
- Use the Language Understanding AI demo to showcase the Language Understanding Intelligence Service (LUIS)
- Utilize other real-life use cases of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and use these cases as examples to explain some of the concepts
