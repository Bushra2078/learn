

AI-900: Module 3 Computer Vision 

As you are preparing to teach this module, get familiar with what the students will learn during the module. In this module, students will be introduced to some of the workloads that are mentioned in Module 1, AI, and computer vision. Students will also explore Computer Vision in Azure. 

This module consists of two lessons: 

- Lesson 1 – Computer Vision concepts 
- Lesson 2 – Creating Computer Vision solutions in Azure
