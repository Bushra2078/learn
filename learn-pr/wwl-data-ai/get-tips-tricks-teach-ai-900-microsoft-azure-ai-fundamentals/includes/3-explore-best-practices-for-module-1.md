As you're preparing to teach this module, get familiar with what the students will learn during the module – the concepts and methodologies they'll be working through. In this module, students will learn about Artificial Intelligence (AI) and the key elements.
 
This module consists of two lessons:

- **Lesson 1** – Artificial Intelligence in Azure
- **Lesson 2** – Responsible AI

> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/videoplayer/embed/RE4XWCm]
 
### Module 1 Tips and Tricks

- Keep this module relatively short as it's an introduction
- Start with the basics – what is AI?
- Introduce the rest of the course at a high-level
- Utilize real-life examples to explain the responsible AI component
  - Consider using Microsoft [customer stories](https://aka.ms/customerstories)
  - Use the responsible AI learning paths and other content that is a part of the [Microsoft AI Business School](https://aka.ms/AIBS)
- Ask students to think about examples of AI they've seen or used in real-life
