

AI-900: Module 1 Explore Fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence 

Module 1 Overview 

As you are preparing to teach this module, get familiar with what the students will learn during the module – the concepts and methodologies they will be working through. In this module students will learn about artificial intelligence (AI) as an overview and Azure agnostic model and discuss some of the different workloads related to it.  

This module consists of two lessons: 

- Lesson 1 – Introduction to Artificial Intelligence 
- Lesson 2 – Artificial Intelligence in Microsoft Azure

Module 1 Tips and Tricks 

- Start with the basics: What is AI? 
- Introduce the rest of the course at a high-level – we will cover AI services such as Machine Learning (ML), Computer Vision (CV), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and bots 
- Find real-life examples to explain the responsible AI component 
- Keep this module relatively short as it is an introduction 
- Ask students to think about examples of AI they have seen or used in real-life
